## Photography notes

- Phone camera is fine. Avoid harsh overhead fluorescents — drag
  the part to a window for hand-bench shots if possible.
- Hero shot wants raking light: shoot 30-60 minutes before sunset
  outside, or move the sign to a window with side light.
- For process shots, prioritize legibility over aesthetics: a
  clear shot of the tool change is more useful than a moody
  blur.
