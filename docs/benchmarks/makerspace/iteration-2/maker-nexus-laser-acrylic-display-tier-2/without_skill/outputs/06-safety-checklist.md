# Pre-Flight Safety Checklist

Sign and date before each work session. If you can't tick a box, stop and ask a steward.

## Personal / facility (every session)

- [ ] I have signed in at the Maker Nexus front desk.
- [ ] I am wearing closed-toe shoes.
- [ ] Long hair tied back, no loose sleeves, no hanging jewelry.
- [ ] I know where the nearest fire extinguisher and first-aid kit are.
- [ ] I know the evacuation route from the room I'm working in.

## Laser room (Session B, step B3)

- [ ] I am cleared on this specific laser cutter (booking confirms training).
- [ ] **The material I am about to cut is verified ACRYLIC (PMMA), not polycarbonate, not PVC, not ABS, not foam, not unlabeled mystery plastic.** If I can't read a label, I do not cut.
- [ ] Bed is clean, honeycomb is undamaged.
- [ ] Exhaust / fume extraction is running and air assist is ON.
- [ ] CO2 fire extinguisher is within arm's reach and unobstructed.
- [ ] Lid will close fully (no part sticking up too tall).
- [ ] I have done a frame / dry run with the laser OFF to confirm position.
- [ ] I will stay at the machine for the entire job — no exceptions.

## Woodshop (Session A)

- [ ] I am cleared on every tool I plan to use today (table saw, router table, drill press, sander).
- [ ] Safety glasses on.
- [ ] Hearing protection for table saw and router.
- [ ] Dust collection ON or shop-vac connected to the tool.
- [ ] No phone in hand while a tool is running.
- [ ] Push sticks / featherboards staged before I start the cut, not mid-cut.
- [ ] Walnut allergy note: walnut dust is a known sensitizer for some makers — wear a respirator (P100 or N95) when sanding for extended periods.

## Electronics bench (Session B, step B5)

- [ ] Soldering iron stand is stable; iron tip is clean and tinned.
- [ ] Ventilation: small fan or fume extractor running.
- [ ] Wet sponge or brass coil ready.
- [ ] Power supply UNPLUGGED while I am soldering its leads.
- [ ] After wiring, multimeter continuity check before applying 12V.

## Material verification — sign here before laser cut
