## Why plasma over waterjet

- Organic curved silhouette is forgiving of plasma's ~0.060" kerf and slight bevel.
- 1/4" mild steel cuts cleanly on Maker Nexus's plasma table; well within capacity.
- Significantly faster than waterjet for this thickness and area (~5-10x throughput).
- Powder coat hides minor dross/edge artifacts; no need for waterjet's superior edge.
- Cost per linear inch of cut is far lower; no abrasive consumable.

Trade-offs accepted: heat-affected zone (HAZ) ~0.010-0.030" wide, slight edge bevel (3-7 deg typical), some dross on bottom edge requiring grinding.
