# 02 - Bill of Materials

## Steel

| Item | Spec | Qty | Source | Est. cost |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Mild steel plate, A36 | 1/4" x 30" x 78" | 1 | IMS / Industrial Metal Supply (Sunnyvale) or SendCutSend remnant; or shop drop stock | $55-90 |

Notes:
- Buy oversize: 30" x 78" gives ~3" margin around the 24" x 72" silhouette for clamping/tab waste.
- A36 is fine. No need for A572 / hi-strength.
- Hot-rolled is acceptable - powder coat hides mill scale once blasted.
- Check shop's remnant rack first - 1/4" plate offcuts are common.
