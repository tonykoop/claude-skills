# 08 - Safety and PPE

## Plasma-specific hazards

| Hazard | Source | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| UV/IR radiation | Plasma arc | **Shade 8-9 cutting goggles or auto-darkening helmet at shade 8.** Sunglasses are NOT sufficient. Bystanders at distance need shade 5 minimum. |
| Hot metal sparks/spatter | Cut zone | Leather welding gloves (full gauntlet), leather apron or FR cotton/wool clothing, leather boots covering ankles. NO synthetic fabrics anywhere - they melt to skin. |
| Inhalation of metal fume | Vaporized steel and any coating | Plasma table downdraft must be ON. Half-face respirator with P100 cartridge over the table. Hexavalent chromium not a concern on plain A36, but iron oxide fume is still a respiratory hazard. |
| Noise | Arc + air rush | Plasma at 65 A is ~95 dBA at the operator. Foam plugs minimum, muffs over plugs preferred. |
| Electric shock | Pilot arc circuit | Don't touch the torch tip when machine is on. Don't change consumables with power on. |
| Fire | Sparks landing on combustibles | Clear 10 ft radius of flammables. Fire extinguisher (ABC) within reach. Slat bed swept clean before cut. |

## Grinding hazards (Stage 6 finishing)

- Face shield over safety glasses for grinding/flap disc work.
- Pull hair back, no loose sleeves, no necklaces or rings.
- Inspect flap disc and wire wheel for cracks/missing wires before each use; never exceed RPM rating.
- Wire wheels eject wires at projectile speed - point grinder so ejected wires fly AWAY from people and into a backstop.

## Powder coating hazards

- Powder is fine particulate; wear N95 minimum during application even in a booth.
- Booth must be grounded and ventilated; never coat in an enclosed space.
- Oven cure releases small amounts of fumes - run booth/oven exhaust during cure.
- Hot part comes out of oven at 400 F - use heat-resistant gloves, never bare or nitrile.

## Lifting

- Finished part is ~28 lb. Awkward 6 ft length, sharp edges (until deburred). Two-person carry while moving from plasma to grinder station.
- Use a wheeled cart for cross-shop moves. Don't drag steel across painted floors - scratches the floor and the part.

## Buddy system

Even though metalshop is cleared for solo work, plasma cutting at this scale benefits from a second pair of eyes during the long outer-profile cut (>20 minutes of cut time). Ask another cleared member to be in earshot.
