# Project Brief — Heavy-Duty Waxed Canvas Zippered Tote

**Maker:** (Maker Nexus member)
**Makerspace:** Maker Nexus (Sunnyvale, CA)
**Build tier:** Tier 2 (intermediate — assumes basic sewing skills, walks through new techniques)
**Date:** 2026-05-08

## Finished dimensions
- Width: 18 in
- Height: 14 in
- Depth (gusset): 6 in
- Drop length (straps): ~10 in (24 in strap length)

## Construction summary
- **Exterior:** 12-oz waxed canvas (single layer, no additional interfacing — the wax-canvas hand is structural enough at 12 oz)
- **Lining:** mid-weight cotton twill (~7-8 oz)
- **Closure:** #5 or #8 metal/coil zipper, 20-22 in (slightly wider than opening so the slider parks past the gusset corner)
- **Straps:** 1.5 in cotton webbing core, wrapped in waxed canvas, padded with 1/4 in closed-cell foam, double-needle topstitched
- **Interior:** one slip pocket (8 x 7 in) on lining back panel
- **Seams:** bound with 1 in cotton bias tape on all exterior structural seams; serged or zigzagged on lining seams

## Why Tier 2
Tier 2 is appropriate because:
- Bound seams add a step beyond standard seam allowances
- Boxed corners + zipper + lining + padded straps is 4 techniques layered together
- Waxed canvas does not behave like quilting cotton (no pinning through the wax — clips only; topstitching marks are permanent)
- Industrial walking-foot use is new equipment for a domestic-cert holder

A Tier 1 packet would skip the zipper or the lining. A Tier 3 packet would assume you can pattern from a sketch.

## Equipment certification path (the question you asked)
You hold the **domestic sewing machine cert**. You want to use the **industrial walking-foot** for the canvas. Here's what Maker Nexus requires (see `04-walking-foot-cert.md` for full detail):

1. **Walking-foot orientation class** — ~90 min, offered most weeks. Prereq: domestic cert (you have it).
2. **Supervised first-use sign-off** — bring a scrap stack of your project material; a steward watches you thread, adjust tension, sew a test seam, and change a needle. ~30 min.
3. **Independent booking** — after sign-off you can reserve the machine in 2-hr blocks via the member portal.

The walking-foot WILL handle 12-oz waxed canvas comfortably (it is rated to ~3/8 in stack of heavy denim/canvas/leather). A domestic machine struggles at the boxed-corner intersections where you stack 4 layers of waxed canvas + bias binding — that's the reason to certify up.
