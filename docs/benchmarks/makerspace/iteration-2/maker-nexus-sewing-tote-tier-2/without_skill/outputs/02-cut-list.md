# Cut List

All measurements are in inches. Seam allowance (SA) included as noted. Cut on grain — for waxed canvas, parallel to the selvedge for the long dimension of each panel.

## Finished bag math
- Exterior body wraps as a single panel: bottom + two sides = 14 + 6 + 14 = 34 in tall before SA
- Front/back width: 18 in + 1 in SA total = 19 in
- Gusset on each end (left/right): 6 in wide x 14 in tall

## Bias tape (binding)
- Total ~4 yards of 1 in finished bias tape
- Cut from lining or contrast cotton at true 45° bias
- Cut strips 2 in wide, join end-to-end with 45° seams, press in half then again to make double-fold

## Cutting notes for waxed canvas
1. **Do not pre-wash.** Wax will redistribute and you'll lose the finish.
2. **Mark on the wrong side only,** with chalk or a hera marker. Pencil lines are permanent.
3. **Cut with a sharp rotary blade.** Scissors crush the wax and leave a fuzzy edge.
4. **No pins.** Use clips, pattern weights, or a tracing wheel into the SA only.
5. Lay the cut panels flat — do not fold sharply. A crease in waxed canvas can be permanent (a heat gun or hair dryer can recover most creases later).
