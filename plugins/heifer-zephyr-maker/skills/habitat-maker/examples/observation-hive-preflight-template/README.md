# Observation-Hive Preflight Template

Status: concept/preflight only. This template is not approved for live colony use.
It is not a fabrication-ready hive plan.

Use this example when a prompt asks for an observation-hive design review,
public-facing family makerspace packet, or camera/electronics caveat around an
observation hive. It gives `habitat-maker` a safe first artifact: route out
beekeeping decisions, record welfare gates, and block premature geometry until
qualified review exists.

## Required Route-Outs

Route these decisions away from the public skill packet before any live use:

- qualified beekeeper review for colony welfare and operating procedures
- local legal compliance, permitting, transport, public display, and nuisance
  requirements
- live bee handling, colony installation/removal, emergency response, and any
  service procedure involving an occupied hive
- colony sourcing, queen status, feeding, disease response, inspection cadence,
  overwintering, and seasonal management
- private family details, recovered archive scans, personal locations, names,
  children/family identities, and unreleased co-design material
- camera, microphone, network, IR, visible light, audio, ultrasonic, battery,
  solar, or sensor decisions unless species-safe evidence and a disable/service
  plan are documented

## Public/Private Boundary

Public packets may describe a family-friendly makerspace goal, review
requirements, non-expert limits, and blank packet slots. They must not include
private media, personal locations, family identities, unreleased archive scans,
or live-colony operating instructions.

## Fabrication Authority

Do not add `geometry_params.json`, CAD, DXF, cut-ready drawings, or generated
visuals to this example. Observation-hive geometry should wait until the frame
standard, viewing layout, service path, containment approach,
ventilation/thermal assumptions, maximum unattended display assumptions, and
qualified-review requirements are selected.

Concept images may support discussion, but CAD/DXF/JSON or reviewed dimensioned
drawings remain the only fabrication authority for future packets.

## Files In This Template

- `README.md` - readiness label, route-outs, public/private boundary, and
  fabrication authority notes
- `welfare-checklist.md` - required observation-hive and electronics gates
- `packet-plan.md` - blocked placeholders for a future reviewed packet
