# Observation-Hive Welfare Checklist

Every gate below is pass/fail. A public packet can remain concept/preflight
only while gates are incomplete, but it must not claim live colony readiness
until all required gates pass with evidence.

| Gate id | Applies to | Severity | Pass condition | Fail remedy | Evidence | Source ref |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `qualified_keeper_review` | `observation_hive_preflight` | required | A qualified local beekeeper has reviewed the packet before any live colony use. | Mark the packet concept-only and remove live-use claims. | review note or signoff record | `bat-bee-observation-hive-welfare` |
| `secure_containment` | `observation_hive_preflight` | required | Bee paths, doors, seals, viewing panels, latches, and frame interfaces are escape-resistant by design. | Redesign closures, gaskets, frame capture, and viewing-panel retention. | drawing review and closure checklist | `bat-bee-observation-hive-welfare` |
| `ventilation_thermal_management` | `observation_hive_preflight` | required | Packet documents airflow, heat load, sun exposure, emergency shade/cooling, and maximum unattended display assumptions. | Add ventilation/thermal analysis or reject the viewing location. | ventilation drawing and heat-risk checklist | `bat-bee-observation-hive-welfare` |
| `escape_proof_service_access` | `observation_hive_preflight` | required | Inspection and service access can be performed without uncontrolled bee release into public or family areas. | Add service protocol constraints or keep the packet as a non-live display. | service-access drawing and reviewed procedure | `bat-bee-observation-hive-welfare` |
| `public_privacy_safety` | `observation_hive_preflight` | required | Packet excludes private family/media details and names public standoff, signage, supervision, and non-expert limits. | Redact private content and add public-safety boundaries. | redaction review and safety checklist | `bat-bee-observation-hive-welfare` |
| `route_out_colony_decisions` | `observation_hive_preflight` | required | Colony sourcing, feeding, disease, queen status, legal, seasonal, and live-handling decisions are explicitly out of scope. | Add a route-out section and qualified-review requirement. | packet route-out section | `bat-bee-observation-hive-welfare` |
| `no_contact_protrusions` | `camera_electronics` | conditional | If electronics are present, no mounts, screws, lenses, ties, or housings protrude into bee-contact paths. | Move hardware outside the enclosure or behind a smooth sealed barrier. | interior inspection | `bat-bee-observation-hive-welfare` |
| `no_exposed_wires` | `camera_electronics` | conditional | If electronics are present, wires are outside bee paths, strain-relieved, and protected from moisture and abrasion. | Externalize wiring or remove electronics. | cable-route drawing | `bat-bee-observation-hive-welfare` |
| `low_heat_load` | `camera_electronics` | conditional | If electronics are present, heat load, sun exposure, ventilation, and worst-case temperature posture are documented. | Use lower-power external devices, shade, or delete electronics. | heat-risk note and device spec | `bat-bee-observation-hive-welfare` |
| `weatherproof_external_routing` | `camera_electronics` | conditional | If electronics are present, cable penetrations shed water and cannot wick moisture into the hive. | Add drip loops, exterior glands, or sealed external raceways. | penetration detail | `bat-bee-observation-hive-welfare` |
| `service_without_disturbance` | `camera_electronics` | conditional | If electronics are present, battery, storage, cleaning, reset, and replacement do not require opening an occupied hive. | Move service points outside or allow service only when confirmed unoccupied. | service plan | `bat-bee-observation-hive-welfare` |
| `species_safe_sensing` | `camera_electronics` | conditional | If electronics are present, lights, IR, audio, ultrasonic, wireless, and heat-emitting devices are absent unless species-safe evidence and a disable plan exist. | Disable emissions, move the sensor outside, or reject the feature. | evidence citation and disable checklist | `bat-bee-observation-hive-welfare` |
| `fabrication_authority` | `camera_electronics` | required | Generated images and concept renders are labeled non-authoritative; future dimensions come from CAD/DXF/JSON or reviewed drawings. | Update packet language before release. | artifact manifest | `bat-bee-observation-hive-welfare` |
