# Risks

## Acoustic

- Risk: coupled behavior differs from isolated formulas.
- Test: compare repeated bench-rig measurements against local string,
  spring, and membrane estimates before advancing maturity.

## Structural

- Risk: anchors, bridges, or frame members slip under stored energy.
- Test: inspect before each run and stop on any creep, crack, or deformation.

## Ergonomic

- Risk: performance layout is designed before the coupled sound is stable.
- Test: keep the packet at `bench_rig` until repeatability gates pass.

## Sensor

- Risk: microphone or contact sensor placement changes the apparent result.
- Test: record mic distance, gain, contact mic mounting, and environment for
  every run.

## Safety

- Risk: string or spring release path crosses operator or equipment.
- Test: use secondary restraints and keep people, cameras, and microphones
  out of the release path.
