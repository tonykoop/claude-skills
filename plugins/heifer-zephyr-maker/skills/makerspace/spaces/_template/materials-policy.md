# Materials policy — example-space

Plain-English statement of what's banned where, and why.

## Universal bans (anywhere in the shop)

- Chlorinated plastics on lasers (PVC, vinyl) — produces hydrochloric
  acid that destroys optics and harms lungs.
- ABS on lasers — cyanide-bearing fumes.
- Polycarbonate on lasers — toxic and doesn't cut cleanly.
- Anything fluoride-bearing on lasers (PTFE / Teflon).

## Tool-specific bans

- **CNC router (example-cnc-1):** banned: aluminum, steel, brass,
  any ferrous metal. Reason: chip-eject contaminates wood-shop dust
  collection; bearings not rated for metal swarf.
- **Tablesaw:** no MDF rip without dust-mask + extraction running.
  Engineered woods generate fine dust that's a long-term lung
  hazard.
