# Safety rules — example-space

Shop-wide safety policy. PPE expectations. Dust collection. Emergency.

## PPE — always

- Safety glasses any time a tool is running.
- Closed-toe shoes anywhere on the shop floor.
- Hearing protection in machine areas.

## PPE — task-dependent

- Dust mask / respirator for sanding, MDF cutting, finishing.
- Cut-resistant gloves around blades — except where rotating
  spindles can grab the glove (lathe, drill press).
- Welding hood + leather apron + leather gloves for welding.

## Dust collection

- Tablesaw, jointer, planer: connect to central dust collection
  before turning on.
- Sanders: dust mask + central collection or shop vac.
- CNC: dust shoe must be installed for any wood operation.
