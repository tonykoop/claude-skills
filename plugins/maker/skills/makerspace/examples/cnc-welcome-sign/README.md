## What it is

An 18 × 6 × ¼-inch baltic birch welcome sign with V-carved relief
letters at 0.125-in depth, sized to fit above a standard 4-inch
mailbox post. Two mounting cleats on the back face. Wipe-on
polyurethane satin finish, two coats.

## Where it was built

Maker Nexus, Sunnyvale CA. Cut on the woodshop CNC; finished in the
finishing area.

## Tools and certifications

- Drill press for mounting holes — requires `woodshop-cert`

## How to build it

1. Buy stock and finishing supplies (see `bom.csv` and `sourcing.csv`).
2. Verify cuts on the cut list against your actual sheet — kerf
   shifts the layout.
3. CAM the V-carve in Vectric VCarve (Maker Nexus's standard tool);
   feeds/speeds in `op-sequence.md`.
4. Run the CNC, then bandsaw the body free of the sheet, sand to
   220, drill mounting holes, finish.
