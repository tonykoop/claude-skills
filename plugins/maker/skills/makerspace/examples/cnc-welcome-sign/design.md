| Length | L | 18 | in | input |

## Critical dimensions

- Outer dims: 18 × 6 × 0.25 in
- Letter relief depth: 0.125 in (half-stock; structural OK)
- Corner radius: 0.25 in (matches a ¼" compression bit's radius)
- Mount holes: ¼" Ø, 0.5 in inset from each long edge, centered
  on the short edges

## Derived dimensions

- h = 0.40 × W = 0.40 × 6 = 2.4 in (letter cap height)
- b = (W − h) / 2 = (6 − 2.4) / 2 = 1.8 in (top and bottom border)
- Effective material remaining under deepest cut:
  t − d = 0.25 − 0.125 = 0.125 in (acceptable for ¼" baltic birch)
