# Classes — Maker Nexus

Class catalog — verify dates and pricing on the official schedule
at https://www.makernexus.org/classes.

## `#class-shop-orientation`

1 hour. Required first. Free with membership (verify).

## `#class-woodshop-intro`

4 hours. Hands-on intro covering:
- Tablesaw safety and basic ripping/crosscutting
- Jointer / planer basics
- Drill press safety
- Bandsaw safety
- Router table basics
- PPE expectations
Grants: `#woodshop-cert`.

## `#class-cnc-intro`

4 hours. Hands-on intro covering:
- CAM workflow (Vectric or Fusion 360)
- Tool selection and feeds/speeds for plywood and hardwood
- Workholding (vacuum table + tabs, fixturing)
- Setting work zero, running the first job
- Diagnosing common failure modes (bit deflection, lost steps,
  workholding failures)
Grants: `#cnc-cert`.

## `#class-laser-intro`

2 hours. Covers:
- Material selection (banned list, allowed list)
- Power/speed/passes calibration
- Focus and air assist
- Vector cut vs raster engrave vs vector score
- Filing a job, fume management
Grants: `#laser-cert`.

## `#class-3dp-fdm`

2 hours. Slicing, bed prep, first-layer adhesion, common failure
modes. Grants: `#3dp-fdm-cert`.

## `#class-3dp-resin`

2 hours. Resin handling, support generation, post-cure, IPA wash,
disposal. Grants: `#3dp-resin-cert`.

## `#class-metalshop-intro`

4 hours. Lathe/mill/welder basics depending on focus. Grants:
`#metalshop-cert`.

## `#class-textile-intro`

2 hours. Domestic and industrial sewing machines, sergers, basic
maintenance. Grants: `#textile-cert`.

## Update cadence
