## Preferred Mounts

- Building wall or masonry, especially where the wall adds thermal stability.
- Paired pole/post frame with two houses back-to-back, predator baffle, and no
  branch access.

## Disallowed Default Placements

- Tree mount.
- Low fence mount.
- Metal building wall without thermal backing.
- Location below branches, roofs, or ledges that let predators reach the roost.
- Site without at least 12 ft of clear drop below the exit.
- Site over doors, seating, grills, walkways, or food prep areas.

## Thermal Worksheet

- Cool or cloudy sites: darker exterior and more sun.
- Warm sites: medium exterior, vents, and morning to mid-day sun.
- Very hot sites: light exterior, afternoon shade, and paired houses or a
  shade structure. Do not deploy without an overheating check.

## Maintenance Timing

Inspect, repair, or repaint only when bats are absent. In many regions this
means winter or after the local maternity season. Never open or rework an
occupied roost.
