# Safety And Welfare Checklist

Do not install the box with unresolved failures.

## Wildlife Gates

- [ ] Entrance measures 1.125 inch with no positive oversize tolerance.
  - Fail remedy: recut the front or add a correctly sized entrance guard.
- [ ] No exterior perch is present.
  - Fail remedy: remove perch and seal exterior-only fastener holes.
- [ ] Interior is bare unfinished wood.
  - Fail remedy: reject finished interior parts or recut untreated plies.
- [ ] Interior front below entrance has roughened or scored climb grip.
  - Fail remedy: add shallow horizontal grooves before final assembly.
- [ ] Cleanout side or roof opens and latches securely.
  - Fail remedy: adjust hinge/latch before field use.
- [ ] Planned mount includes predator protection, preferably a baffled smooth pole.
  - Fail remedy: install baffle before nesting season.
- [ ] Planned site is within about 30 feet of woody cover and faces away from prevailing storm wind.
  - Fail remedy: relocate the mount.

## Fabrication Gates

- [ ] Stock is untreated, unpainted, dry cedar or verified laser-safe exterior plywood.
  - Fail remedy: replace stock; do not cut treated, painted, unknown, MDF, PVC, or mystery reclaimed material.
- [ ] Laser kerf test coupon measures 1.000 +/- 0.005 inch after compensation.
  - Fail remedy: adjust CAM kerf offset and repeat.
- [ ] All three plies of each panel align before glue-up.
  - Fail remedy: sort, recut, or sand alignment issues before applying glue.
- [ ] Glue squeeze-out is absent from interior faces, entrance, vent slots, and drainage notches.
  - Fail remedy: clear before cure or reject the affected panel.
- [ ] Screw points do not protrude into the nest cavity.
  - Fail remedy: change screw length or trim/cover hardware.

## Functional Tests

- [ ] Drainage test: pour 250 mL water into the empty upright box; visible pooling clears within 60 seconds.
  - Fail remedy: reopen corner notches and verify floor slope/recess.
- [ ] Airflow test: smoke or fog near the entrance visibly exits side vent slots.
  - Fail remedy: clear slots or recut side plies.
- [ ] Cleanout test: open, close, and latch the access panel three times without binding or rattling.
  - Fail remedy: adjust hinges/latch.
