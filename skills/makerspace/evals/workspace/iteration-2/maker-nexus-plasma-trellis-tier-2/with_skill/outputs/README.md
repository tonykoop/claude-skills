# Climbing-Vine Plasma Trellis — Maker Nexus Tier 2 Packet

A 6-foot-tall outdoor garden trellis cut from 1/4" mild steel on the
plasma table at Maker Nexus. The silhouette is an organic climbing-vine
pattern — long sinuous stem with cardioid leaves layered along its
length — chosen specifically because its smooth curves play to plasma's
strength and don't reward the higher precision of a waterjet at the cost
of speed and material loss.

Powder-coat finish: matte black (RAL 9005 texture or equivalent).

## Project intent

- One-piece structural silhouette, no welded joinery on the leaves.
- 6 ft (72") tall × 24" wide × 1/4" thick — visible from both faces.
- Two ground spikes welded to the bottom rail for in-soil mounting.
- Designed to be cut, deburred, blast-prepped, powder-coated, and
  installed in a single shop weekend.
