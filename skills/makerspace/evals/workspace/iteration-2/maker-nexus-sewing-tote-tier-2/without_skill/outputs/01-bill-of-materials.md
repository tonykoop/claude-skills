# Bill of Materials

All quantities include ~10% waste/error margin. Costs are 2026 Bay Area estimates.

## Fabric
| Item | Spec | Qty | Where | Est. cost |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Waxed canvas, exterior | 12 oz, 58-60 in wide, color of choice (field tan, olive, charcoal common) | 1 yd | Stonemountain & Daughter (Berkeley), A.L. Frances, Big Duck Canvas online | $28-38 |
| Cotton twill, lining | 7-8 oz, 54-58 in wide, color to contrast | 1 yd | Stonemountain, Britex, Joann | $10-16 |

## Hardware & notions
| Item | Spec | Qty | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Metal zipper | #5 brass or #8 coil, 22 in, separating NOT required | 1 | YKK preferred; Klum House and Wawak both stock |
| Cotton webbing | 1.5 in wide, natural or black, for strap core | 2 yd | Mid-weight (not the floppy craft stuff) |
| Closed-cell foam | 1/4 in thick, sew-in or iron-on | 1 strap-length (24 in x 3 in) | EVA or similar; do NOT use polyester batting — it compresses |
| Bias tape, cotton | 1 in single-fold or 2 in double-fold cut to 1 in finish | 4 yd | Make your own from leftover lining for nicest result |
| Polyester thread | Tex 40 / V-46 (heavy-duty), color to match exterior | 1 large spool (~500 yd) | Gutermann Mara 70, Coats Dual Duty XP Heavy, or Sulky 12wt-equivalent |
| Topstitching thread (optional) | Tex 70 / V-69 contrast | 1 small spool | For decorative topstitch on straps and zipper tape |
| Snap or magnetic closure (optional interior) | 14 mm magnetic snap | 1 set | For the slip pocket if desired |

## Maker Nexus shop supplies (likely free/included)
- Wonder clips (do NOT use pins on waxed canvas — leaves permanent holes)
- Tailor's chalk or hera marker (no friction pens — heat sets the wax)
- Walking-foot machine needles: **schmetz 100/16 jeans or 110/18 leather** (bring your own or ask the steward)
- Rotary cutter, mat, long quilting ruler

## Total estimated cost
**~$55-90** for materials, depending on fabric choices. Hardware ~$8-12.
